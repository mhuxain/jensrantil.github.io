Refreshable AngularjS service

Mon 20 May 2013

Lately I've been working a lot in AngularJS_. This is my second project with the framework, and the more time I spend in it, the more I like it.

But as with most things, AngularJS has some rough corners. Its documentation_ still has a long way to go, altough it has improved since last time I used it over a year ago. Common use cases and "Angular-y" was of doing things is another thing that I also feel is lacking out there. I guess it could be considered documentation depending on how you look at it.

My intention with this blog post is to go through one such use case, namely dealing with complex dependencies from API endpoints.
