Bootstrapping: CouchDB as event store

I've previously summarized what CQRS and event store is. Hearing/reading about it, you might wonder "heck, sounds great! But how do I get started?". This is a great question. This blog post will propose a simple way to get started.

Minimum viable product

First, let's start about how to start a project. I'm a big fan of the concept of "minimum viable product". If you start a project, you have no idea whether it is going to be a successful one or not. Is it ever going to be profitable? Am I tackling things the right way? Is there demand for this kind of product? There are very few cases when you really know.

Things brings us to our second insight. With the little that we usually know about a future project, we also don't know very much about its requirements. How much will it need to scale? In what direction will the project move in the long run? There are many cases when a business model fails, but while failing the company realizes there's an opening in another business model space!
